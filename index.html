EcosEirene

made by kelompok 6

"EcosEirene" comes from the Greek language which is "Ecos" which means Ecosystem and "Eirene" which means Peace. So if put together "EcosEirene" has the meaning of a Clean Ecosystem Environment, it will bring peace. Why do we choose this? Because with a clean ecosystem or environment, it will bring peace to anyone who lives in the environment itself. and also because we love to live in peace.
